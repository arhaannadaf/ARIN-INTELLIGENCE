# Objective

1. The objective of the Arin Intelligence  are to systematically analyze economic news and publicly available economic indicators in order to exact meaningful, data driven insights. The project aims to organize unstructured news data into a structured format that can be combined with quantitative economic matrics for analysis

2. It is used to identify observable trends, sentiment patterns, and relationships within economic data using exploratory analysis and visualization techniques. The project focuses on presenting these insights in a clear and interpretable manner rather than relying on complex predictive models.

3. Arin Intelligence seeks to maintain a reproductive and well- documented analytical workflow, ensuring that the methodology and results can be easily understood, reviewed, extended in the future.
