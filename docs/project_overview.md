# Overview
ARIN Intelligence is a Data Analysis project focused on understanding economic news and publicly available indicators. 

## AIM
It Aims to conert unstructures news content into structures data and analyze it alongside quantitative metrics to identify patterns, sentiments shifts, and observable trends.

## Goal
Its Primary goal is to bridge narrative-based economic reporting with data-driven analysis. By applying systematic data cleaning, exploratory analysis and clear visualization, the project presents economic information in a form that is easier to interpret and reason about.

The project emphasizes claity, reproductibility, and insights generation rather than complex predictive modeling, making it suitable for analytical evaluation and practical understanding of real world economic behaviour.
