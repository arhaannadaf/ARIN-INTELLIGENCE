# Results

This section presents the key findings obtained from the analysis conducted in Arin Intelligence. The results are derived from the combined examination of economic news content and publicly available economic indicators.

## Exploratory Analysis Findings

Exploratory data analysis revealed observable patterns in economic indicators across time periods and regions. Trends related to inflation, growth, and employment showed variations that aligned with major economic events reported in the news data.

## Sentiments Analysis Outcomes

Sentiment analysis of econpomic news articles indiacated noticeable shifts in narrtive tone during periods of economic uncertainty and policy changes. These sentiment patterns, while approximate, provided contextual signals that complemented quantitative indicators.

## Economic Trend Insights

When analyzed together, sentiment trends and economic indicators highlighted relationshi between public economic narrative and measurable economic movements. While these relationship do not imply causation, they offer useful perspectives for understanding border economic behaviours.

## Visualization Summary

Visulalization such as ine charts and comparative plots were effective in communication changes in indicators and sentiment over time. Thse visual summaries improved interpretability and supported clearer reasoning about observed trends.

## ROI Perspective

The return on investment of Arin Intelligence is assessed from an analytical and decision-support standpoint rather than direct financial gain. The project demonstrates value by reducing manual effort in interpreting large volumes of economic news and data, improving consistency, and enabling faster insight generation through structured analysis and visualization.

### Results Limitation

The results should be interpreted with caution due to limuitations in data coverage, sentiment anlaysis accuracy, and the exploratory nature of the analysis. Findings are indicative and intended to support understanding rather than prediction or finanical decision- making.
